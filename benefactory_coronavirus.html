Donate to sponsor masks for patients and healthcare professionals on the front line at government hospitals in Mumbai to contain the pandemic.




Maharashtra has the highest number of positive coronavirus cases in India. And it is very likely that the number of cases is much higher than what is being declared given that India has only 10 tests per MILLION.

Doctors, nurses are in desperate need for surgical masks as there has been a huge shortage of masks. Civilians have contributed to this shortage for health workers as they have been hoarding masks. These masks designed to be worn only once, but many healthcare workers are using a single-use mask for 5 days which is making the situation worse. Right now, they need more gear to guard against infection.

Masks are required for those who have symptoms of coronavirus or who have tested positive for it. They are also used for current patients in hospitals who are extremely compromised without one; we mean, cancer patients, stroke patients, etc who have an extremely low immunity.

  • Currently, government hospitals are facing a massive shortage of masks for their health care professionals as well as their patients. These hospitals need 1 lakh masks for the next 10 days. However, this may increase depending on the situation.
